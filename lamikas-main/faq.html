Getting Started

How do I get started with LAMIKAS?

Getting started is easy! Simply:

  1. Sign up for a free 7-day trial (no credit card required)
  2. Add your properties and units
  3. Invite your tenants or add them manually
  4. Set up your payment preferences (M-Pesa, bank, card)
  5. Start managing your properties efficiently!

Our onboarding wizard will guide you through each step.

How long does setup take?

Most users can complete basic setup in 15-30 minutes. The time depends on:

  • Number of properties you're adding
  • How many tenants you have
  • Whether you're importing existing data

Our guided setup makes the process quick and easy.

Can I import my existing data?

Yes! We support importing data from:

  • Excel/CSV files
  • Other property management software
  • Spreadsheets or manual records

Contact our support team for assistance with data migration.

Pricing & Billing

What's included in the free trial?

The 7-day free trial includes:

  • Access to all features
  • Unlimited properties and tenants
  • Full payment processing capabilities
  • Email and chat support
  • Mobile app access

No credit card is required to start your trial. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time during or after the trial.

How does billing work?

Billing is simple and transparent:

  • Monthly or annual billing options
  • Billed per unit per month
  • No setup fees or hidden charges
  • Payment via M-Pesa, card, or bank transfer
  • Automatic receipts for all payments

You can change your plan or cancel at any time.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll receive a prorated credit or charge for the remainder of your billing period.

To change your plan:

  1. Go to Account Settings
  2. Click on "Billing"
  3. Select "Change Plan"
  4. Choose your new plan
Are there any transaction fees?

We charge minimal transaction fees for payment processing:

  • M-Pesa: 1% (capped at KSh 100)
  • Cards: 2.9% + KSh 10
  • Bank Transfers: Free

These fees are charged to the tenant or property owner based on your settings. You can choose who pays the transaction fees.

What happens after my free trial?

After your 7-day free trial:

  • You'll receive a reminder email 3 days before the trial ends
  • You can choose to upgrade to a paid plan
  • If you don't upgrade, your account will be paused
  • Your data is preserved for 30 days
  • You can reactivate at any time by choosing a plan

No charges will be applied without your consent.

Payments

Which payment methods are supported?

We support multiple payment methods tailored for Africa:

  • M-Pesa: Direct integration with instant confirmation
  • Cards: Visa, MasterCard, and American Express
  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank deposits
  • Cash: Manual payment recording

Tenants can pay through their tenant portal using their preferred method.

How are payments processed?

Payments are processed securely and efficiently:

  1. Tenant initiates payment through their portal
  2. Payment is processed through our secure gateway
  3. Funds are verified and recorded instantly
  4. Both tenant and landlord receive confirmation
  5. Payment appears in your dashboard immediately

Bank transfers may take 1-2 business days to reflect.

Security & Privacy

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously:

  • Bank-level encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Regular backups: Daily backups to secure servers
  • Access controls: Role-based permissions
  • Compliance: GDPR and local data protection laws
  • Security audits: Regular third-party security testing
Who owns my data?

You own your data. We are simply the custodians.

  • You retain full ownership of all your property and tenant data
  • You can export your data at any time
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties
  • You can delete your account and all associated data

Our privacy policy provides full details about data ownership and usage.
